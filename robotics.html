About FIRST Inspires

FIRST is an international organization that organizes the events where approximately 50 teams from an area will meet to play against each other.

FIRST designs the challenges and rules that regulate how the teams can play and focuses on Gracious Professionalism where you encourage the work that others have done and in turn they help you.


6181 Crypic Cyborgs

I participate in a FIRST robotics team at Northwest State Community College called the Crypic Cyborgs.

In the 2022 Season we placed 8th in the Northern Ohio Area out of 61 teams using our robot Rex.

Challenges in the 2022 Season included:

  • Throwing 9.5 inch balls 8 feet into the air into hoops.
  • Hanging your ~150 pound robot from one of 4 different bars near your starting area.

My role on the team is to do a mixture of physical electrical work, and doing programming to control the robot.
